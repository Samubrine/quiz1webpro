Discover Malang

The Apple City

Malang is a charming city nestled in the mountains of East Java, Indonesia. Known as "Kota Apel" (Apple City), Malang is famous for its cool climate, colonial architecture, and vibrant student life. The city serves as a perfect blend of historical heritage and modern urban development.

Cultural Heritage

The city is rich in cultural diversity, home to numerous universities that attract students from across Indonesia. Malang's colonial past is evident in its Dutch-era buildings, while traditional Javanese culture thrives alongside modern influences, creating a unique atmospheric charm.

Natural Beauty & Tourism

Surrounded by mountains and blessed with a cool tropical highland climate, Malang offers stunning natural attractions including waterfalls, temples, and scenic viewpoints. The city serves as a gateway to popular destinations like Mount Bromo and various agro-tourism sites, making it a beloved destination for both domestic and international travelers seeking authentic Indonesian experiences.
