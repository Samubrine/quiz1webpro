Amazing Destinations

Kajoetangan Heritage

A historic colonial street in Malang featuring well-preserved Dutch architecture and heritage buildings. This charming heritage district offers visitors a glimpse into Malang's colonial past with its vintage buildings, antique shops, and cultural attractions that showcase the city's rich historical legacy.

Historical Cultural Heritage
Tumpak Sewu Waterfall

A magnificent curtain waterfall known as "Thousand Waterfalls" featuring multiple streams cascading down from a 120-meter cliff. Located near Lumajang border, this breathtaking natural wonder offers spectacular views and adventurous hiking trails through lush tropical rainforest.

Natural Wonder Adventure
Teluk Asmara Beach

A pristine coastal destination in Malang offering stunning ocean views and tranquil beach atmosphere. This hidden gem features crystal-clear waters, soft sandy shores, and dramatic cliff formations, providing the perfect setting for relaxation and romantic sunset viewing.

Beach Paradise Romantic
Museum Brawijaya

A military museum dedicated to preserving the history of Indonesian independence struggle, particularly focusing on the Brawijaya military division. The museum houses historical artifacts, weapons, uniforms, and documents that chronicle Indonesia's fight for freedom and military heritage.

Military History Educational
Gateway to Adventure

Malang stands as East Java's premier tourist destination, offering an incredible diversity of attractions that cater to every traveler's dreams. From the cool mountain air and cascading waterfalls to modern theme parks and cultural museums, the city provides endless opportunities for exploration and discovery. Whether you're seeking family fun, natural wonders, cultural insights, or thrilling adventures, Malang's strategic location and rich tourism infrastructure make it the perfect base for unforgettable Indonesian experiences.
