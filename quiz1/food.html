Cita Rasa Autentik Malang

Discover Local Flavors

Puthu Lanang

A traditional Javanese steamed rice cake delicacy made from ground rice flour and grated coconut, steamed in bamboo cylinders. This sweet treat is typically served with palm sugar sauce or grated coconut, offering a delightfully soft texture and authentic Indonesian flavor.

★★★★★ Traditional Sweet
Orem Orem

A hearty and flavorful dish combining soft lontong rice cakes with fresh bean sprouts, crispy fried tofu, and savory lentho, perfectly drizzled with sweet soy sauce and topped with crispy fried onions.

★★★★☆ Street Food Favorite
Bakso Malang

Malang's signature meatball soup featuring various types of meatballs including beef, chicken, and fish balls served in a rich, savory broth. Complete with yellow noodles, vegetables, fried wontons, and special sambal, this hearty dish represents the pinnacle of Indonesian comfort food.

★★★★★ Comfort Food Champion
Culinary Heritage of Malang

Malang's culinary landscape reflects the rich cultural tapestry of East Java, where traditional Javanese flavors meet Dutch colonial influences and modern innovations. Each dish tells a story of generations-old recipes passed down through families, combining fresh local ingredients with time-honored cooking techniques. From bustling street food stalls to family-run warungs, Malang offers an authentic taste of Indonesian gastronomy that captivates both locals and visitors alike.
